FAQs

We know you might have a lot of questions! Here are some answered

What's the dress code?

Smart traditional or ethnic wear is encouraged. Think kurtas, sarees, lehengas, or sherwanis. Please avoid wearing white or black.

Is there an airport nearby?

The nearest airport to Cheruthuruthi is Calicut International Airport (~80 km). Cochin International Airport (~100 km) is another option with more frequent flights.

How do I get to the venue?

The River Retreat is in Cheruthuruthi, Thrissur. Cabs are available from Thrissur town (~30 km). We'll share a detailed route map closer to the date.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes, ample parking is available at the venue. For the reception at The Date Banquets, parking is available near Metro Pillar No. 52-55.

Can I take photos?

Absolutely! We'd love you to capture the memories. Please be mindful of the professional photographers and avoid blocking their shots during key moments.

What do I gift the couple?

Your presence is the best gift! If you'd like to give something, a heartfelt note or a contribution to our honeymoon fund would be deeply appreciated.
